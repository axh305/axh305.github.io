Social Action Poster Design

Amid the COVID-19 pandemic which originated in Wuhan, China and made its way to the United States in less than 3 months, this poster tackles the racism, discrimmination, and violence targeted at Chinese-Americans living in the United States. Blunt, though truthful, this poster aims to shed light on the verbal abuses thrown at Chinese-Americans daily as well as to take back these slurs and offensive phrases.
